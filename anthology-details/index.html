Fantasy Romance Anthology Submission Details




Cover Art



Minor changes may be made in the final version



Deadline: August 31st, 2022


Publication: Early 2023 (most likely in February)


Word count: 25,000 maximum, no minimum (compensation varies)


Theme: We are accepting all fantasy romance stories, including those with dark elements and spicy scenes. The cover has been provided to offer inspiration and the general vibe of the anthology; however all cover elements do not need to be included in your story, i.e. your story does not need to have a sword, a rose, and/or winged characters.


Exclusivity: All submissions must be unpublished. If chosen, all stories will be exclusive to the anthology for 1 year from date of publication.


Compensation: Selected stories between 10,000-25,000 words will receive $50.
ALL selected authors will receive a physical copy of the final book.


How to submit: After reading through the FAQ section below, you will find a link that redirects to the submission form. Stories must be in a Google Doc form OR in a Word document.



Frequently Asked Questions:

Do I have to pay anything to be included?

Absolutely not! Contributing authors will never be asked to pay for anything related to book production or advertisements.


Can I purchase author copies?

Yes! You may purchase up to 5 copies of the book at the publisher's price. Near publication, we will send a form to sign up for author copies. After receiving payment, we will send the copies you requested. This is completely optional, and the ONLY time we would ever request money from contributing authors.


Will I be paid?

Yes! All selected authors with stories between 10,000-25,000 words will receive a flat-rate payment of $50 + a physical copy of the finished book. Any selections under 10,000 words will not receive monetary payment, but will receive a physical copy of this finished book.


Will the book be marketed?

Yes! The marketing plan has not been finalized, but we plan to do various things to aid in the success of the book. While we would like contributing authors to be involved with marketing, it will only involve free methods. All paid advertisements will be covered by the publisher.


Does my story need to be edited before I submit it?

Authors are encouraged to submit the best version of their story, so self-editing is strongly advised. Twenty-Two Bloom is not responsible for any editorial services you received prior to submission or unrequested by the publisher.


Can I submit a story with sex?

The intended audience for the anthology is 18+ years old, so sex and other adult themes are allowed; however, we will not include any story that is considered erotica.


Can I submit more than once?

Yes, we will accept two submissions per author; however, we will only include one story per author in the anthology.


Will you own rights to my story forever?

Twenty-Two Bloom will have exclusive publishing rights to your story for one year following the date of publication. After that date, you may publish your story elsewhere. Please note, while we will no longer hold exclusive rights to your story, the anthology will still be available for purchase.


Does the anthology have a title yet?

No, the title will be chosen after stories have been selected. All suggestions are welcome!


Any additional questions can be sent to contact@twentytwobloom.com
